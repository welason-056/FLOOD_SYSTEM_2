Flood Safety Instructions

Before Flooding

  • 📦 Prepare emergency food and clean water (3-day supply)
  • 📄 Keep important documents in waterproof bags
  • 🔋 Charge phones and emergency devices
  • 🏠 Know your nearest evacuation center
  • 📻 Keep a battery-powered radio

During Flooding

  • ⛰️ Move to higher ground immediately
  • 🚶 Avoid walking through flood water (6" can knock you down)
  • ⚡ Disconnect electricity if safe to do so
  • 📱 Follow official government alerts
  • 🚗 Never drive through flooded roads
