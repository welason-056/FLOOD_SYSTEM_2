About the System

Protecting Communities Through
Intelligent Flood Prediction

FloodGuard AI is an innovative AI-powered early warning system designed to predict and mitigate flood risks in Dar es Salaam's most vulnerable communities.

Our Mission

To empower flood-prone communities in Dar es Salaam with accurate, timely, and actionable flood predictions using Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, reducing disaster risks and saving lives.

Our Vision

A flood-resilient Tanzania where communities are equipped with intelligent early warning systems that enable proactive disaster preparedness and response, minimizing the impact of flood disasters.

How FloodGuard AI Works

1. Data Collection

The system collects real-time weather data including rainfall, temperature, humidity, and river water levels from trusted meteorological sources.

2. AI Analysis

Our advanced Machine Learning algorithm analyzes the data, identifies patterns, and predicts flood risks with over 95% accuracy for each community.

3. Instant Alerts

When a high flood risk is detected, the system immediately sends alerts to community members, emergency services, and local authorities for swift action.
